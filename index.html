About the academy

Holistic cricket development built on science-backed drills.

Our mission is to create resilient, technically sound cricketers by combining biomechanics, mental conditioning, and relentless repetition. Every batch is capped to ensure personal attention while detailed progress reports keep parents and players aligned on goals.
